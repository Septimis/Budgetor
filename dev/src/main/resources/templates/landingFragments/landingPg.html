Free

Nothing steals momentum in starting a budget quite like having to pay for budgeting tools. That's why budgetor will be free now and forever. Server costs are paid for by generous donations and selling cosmetic designs and layouts within the website.

Data Secure

Your data is important. Budgetor will never sell or distribute your information. You can read more about how Budgetor handles your privacy here.

Convenient

One of the main goals of Budgetor is to relieve you of the tedious parts of maintaining a budget and deliver a comprehensive overview of your financial situation. This is why budgetor is available across all of your devices with a simple interface to make budgeting a breeze!

My Statement to You.

The last thing you need to worry about when stressing about money is paying for budgeting software. This software is a passion project to help anyone track and make sense of their situation. Take the busy work out of maintaining a budget and focus on the big picture goals that help you achieve financial freedom.
